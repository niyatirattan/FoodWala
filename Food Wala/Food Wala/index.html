Serving reinvented authentic continental dishes with a delicious set menu. Foodwala takes pride in its offerings with a mission to provide food lovers a range of scrumptious dishes served impeccably in a high tech atmosphere at down-to-earth prices.

  • Highest standards in service is the hallmark of this chain.
  • Fitted with the best of equipment using all the latest technologies.
  • Food is cooked in the most hygienic atmosphere using the freshest of ingredients in a state-of- the-art kitchen.

Why Choose Foodwala?

A pleasant and comfortable atmosphere without unwanted disturbances. Quick service at all fronts: from being seated to receiving the cheque & everything in between.
